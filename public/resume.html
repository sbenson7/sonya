Sonya M. Balsara

Sonya Balsara is an actress, singer, model, and artist based in New York City. A recent graduate of NYU Tisch School of the Arts, she studied Musical Theatre at the New Studio on Broadway, film acting at Stonestreet studios and entertainment business at the Stern School of Business and Steinhardt.
